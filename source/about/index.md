For the past 10 years I've written video game reviews for [GameCritics.com](http://www.gamecritics.com) (here's [a link to my work](http://gamecritics.com/tera) specifically). I've also written for the now-defunct Monsters At Play and can also be found at [Addicted to Horror Movies.](http://addictedtohorrormovies.com)

Sweet Perdition is for all the pieces that don't fit anywhere else: essays, reviews of things that aren't video games or horror movies, random things I'm up to. Writing non-reviews will be weird at first--look, I've been writing them a *long time,* okay?--but hopefully this blog will be fun for everyone involved.
